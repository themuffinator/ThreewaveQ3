Threewave Arena
 g_gametype 11

Overview

A Clan Arena style gametype where teams battle to the death on nearly any type of level. Teams spawn with a full compliment of weapons and armor much like in CaptureStrike. The team that destroys the other team first wins.

Info and Rules

- Players compete in timed rounds to eliminate the other team. 
- Matches typically last for 9 rounds on the same level to decide the winning team.
- Players spawn with select ammo and armor.
- Matches can be played on RA3 levels, but only in the Clan Arena section of the map.
