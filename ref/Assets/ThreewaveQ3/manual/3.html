Threewave One Flag CTF
 g_gametype 5

Overview

Quake 3 CTF played with only one flag. The single flag spawns in the middle of the map, and it's either teams objective to gain possession of the flag and take it to the enemy base to score at their normal flag location.

Info and Rules

- Flag stays where it is when dropped, touching it picks it up. 
- Flag must be taken to enemy flag location to score.
- When a team scores, the flag immediately respawns in the middle of the map.

Other Information

- One Flag CTF follows most of the same rules and gameplay as Quake 3 CTF
